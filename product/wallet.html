How chips work

Chips are the in-app currency for tables and tournaments.

1
Buy chips
Top up instantly with card or wallet. Chips land in your Main balance.
2
Play tables & tournaments
Sit at a club table or enter an event using your chips as the buy-in.
3
Winnings move real chips
Win a match and the pot's chips move to your balance, minus a small table rake.
4
Cash out
Convert eligible chips back out, subject to your club's rules and verification.
Club chips vs. Main chips. Club chips stay within a club's economy; Main chips are freely transferable and cash-out eligible after verification.

Responsible play

Only play with what you can comfortably afford. Chips have no guaranteed cash value and outcomes involve skill and chance.

Set deposit and session limits any time, or take a break with a cooling-off period.
