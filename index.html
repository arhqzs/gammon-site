The Engine

Point your camera at the board. Get the answer.

Take a photo of any position — over the board or on screen — and our engine reads it, sets it up, and tells you the best play and the pip count instantly. The strength of a top engine, in your pocket.

  • Snap a picture and the position uploads itself — no fiddly setup.
  • XG-class evaluation built on a tuned GNU core — fast on phone and server.
  • Pip counter built right in — calibrate the board once, count from your tablet.
  • A clean position editor for setting up and sharing studies in seconds.
